# Salvation By Grace Through Faith

This lesson is intended to explain why salvation is by faith and not by doing good works.

### Definitions

**FAITH** — trust and confidence in God. It is obedient action based on:

1. **The Nature of God** — He cannot change (James 1:17), He cannot fail (Job 42:2), and He cannot lie (Titus 1:2).
2. **The Redemptive Work of Christ** (Hebrews 12:2; 1 Corinthians 1:30).
3. **The Word of God** (Jeremiah 1:12; Matthew 24:35).

**GRACE** — God's unmerited favor. Even though we were sinners and deserving judgment, God looked upon us in love and forgave us. It is expressed in what we receive from God, such as forgiveness and restored relationship with Him.

### Salvation: By Grace Through Faith...

#### 1. Leaves Out Boasting Because of Works

> For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith — and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God — not by works, so that no one can boast. — Ephesians 2:8-9

> I do not set aside the grace of God, for if righteousness could be gained through the law, Christ died for nothing! — Galatians 2:21

#### 2. Should Not Be Changed

> I am astonished that you are so quickly deserting the one who called you by the grace of Christ and are turning to a different gospel — which is really no gospel at all. Evidently some people are throwing you into confusion and are trying to pervert the gospel of Christ. But even if we or an angel from heaven should preach a gospel other than the one we preached to you, let them be under God's curse! — Galatians 1:6-8

#### 3. Abraham Himself was Justified by Faith

Consider Abraham:

> He believed God, and it was credited to him as righteousness. — Galatians 3:6

#### 4. Relation of the Law (Works) with Faith

> Is the law, therefore, opposed to the promises of God? Absolutely not! For if a law had been given that could impart life, then righteousness would certainly have come by the law. — Galatians 3:21

> Before the coming of this faith, we were held in custody under the law, locked up until the faith that was to come would be revealed. So the law was our guardian until Christ came that we might be justified by faith. Now that this faith has come, we are no longer under a guardian. So in Christ Jesus you are all children of God through faith. — Galatians 3:23-26

### Think About These

If salvation is by good works:

1. How many good works will a man do in order to be saved?
2. Who will judge that a man has done enough good works?

Salvation is God's gift, received through faith alone. Trust in Christ's finished work — not your own.
